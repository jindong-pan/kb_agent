## Three note types in Zettelkasten

Zettelkasten uses three distinct note types, each with a different
purpose and lifespan.

Fleeting notes are raw captures — quick thoughts, voice memos,
highlights during reading. They are temporary and should be
processed within a day or two into permanent notes, then discarded.

Literature notes summarize what a specific source says, written
entirely in your own words. One note per book, paper, or article.
They live in the literature/ folder and are not ingested into the
vector database because they contain other people's ideas, not yours.

Permanent notes are the core of the system. Each contains one
atomic idea in your own words, written as if for a reader with
no context. These are what get ingested and retrieved.
