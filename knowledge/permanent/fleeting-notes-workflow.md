## Fleeting notes workflow

Fleeting notes are captured freely throughout the day with no
concern for quality. Their only job is to not lose an idea before
you can properly process it.

The processing ritual happens once per day, ideally at the same
time. You read each fleeting note and ask: is this worth keeping?
If yes, rewrite it as a permanent note in your own words. If no,
delete it. The fleeting note is then discarded either way.

This daily rhythm prevents the fleeting folder from becoming a
graveyard of unprocessed thoughts — which defeats the purpose
of the system. Unprocessed notes are not knowledge; they are
just stored anxiety.

A good rule: if you would feel no loss deleting a fleeting note
after two weeks, you never needed it.
