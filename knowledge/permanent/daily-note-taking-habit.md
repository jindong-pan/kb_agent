## Building a daily note-taking habit

A Zettelkasten only compounds in value if notes are added consistently.
The minimum viable habit is 15 minutes per day: 5 minutes capturing
fleeting notes throughout the day, 10 minutes at the end processing
one or two of them into permanent notes.

The common mistake is trying to process everything. You do not need
to turn every fleeting note into a permanent note. One high-quality
permanent note per day, written carefully in your own words and linked
to existing notes, compounds faster than ten low-quality dumps.

After one year of one note per day, you have 365 linked atomic ideas.
After three years, the emergent connections between notes begin to
generate research directions and writing structures you did not plan.

The habit is more important than the volume.
